Apple Cinnamon Zucchini Muffins

Description

Cinnamon Apple Muffins are so scrumptious and full of warm cinnamon and crisp apple bits and finished with a cinnamon sugar topping. They are so damn delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 apples cubed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinammon
  • 1 whole zuchinni grated
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of black cardomom

Steps

  1. Sift flour and mix dry ingredients
  2. Mix wet ingredients together along with spices
  3. Mix wet into dry and put into muffin tins
  4. Bake at 400 degrees F for 18 minutes
