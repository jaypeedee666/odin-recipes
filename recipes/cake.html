Chocolate Cake

Description

A scrumptious, heavenly chocolate cake that is easy to make in just a few hours.

Perfect for a birthday, special occasion or even a wedding!

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of chocolate
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together
  2. Mix wet ingredients together
  3. Heat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Combine ingredients in a cake pan and bake for 35 minutes
